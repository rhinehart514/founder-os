# Value Prop Patterns

## Bad Examples

**Feature list:**
> "AI-powered platform with automated workflows and intelligent routing"
Why bad: describes the product, not the outcome. Customer can't repeat this.

**Generic benefit:**
> "Save time and money on marketing"
Why bad: applies to every product ever made. Zero differentiation.

**Founder language:**
> "GBP-to-site pipeline with convergent AI knowledge base"
Why bad: the customer has never said these words and never will.

**Too long:**
> "We help growing trades companies capture more leads through AI-powered phone answering, automated text follow-ups, and professionally managed websites that stay updated without any effort from you"
Why bad: 30+ words. Nobody repeats this. If you can't say it in one breath, it's not a value prop.

## Good Examples

**Outcome-first, specific:**
> "Your phone gets answered every time — even at 2am. $199/mo."
Why good: outcome (answered), specificity (2am), anchor (price). Customer can repeat this.

**Cost displacement:**
> "Stop paying Angi $100/lead to compete with 4 other contractors."
Why good: names the alternative, quantifies the pain, implies the switch.

**Peer-repeatable:**
> "I got this thing that answers my phone when I'm on a job. Knows my services, books appointments. Two hundred bucks."
Why good: this is what the customer actually says to a friend. Natural, specific, includes price.

## The Pressure Test

Every value prop must pass three checks:

1. **Repeatable** — can a customer say it back unprompted in <10 seconds?
2. **Outcome-first** — does it lead with what changes for them, not what the product does?
3. **Peer-worthy** — would they say it to a colleague without you in the room?

Score each: ✓ pass / ✗ fail. If any fails, rewrite.

## Structure

```
"[Outcome the customer wants] — [without / instead of / for] [anchor to current pain or cost]"
```

Examples:
- "Never miss another call — for less than your worst Angi lead costs"
- "Professional website that stays current — without you ever logging in"
- "Every estimate gets followed up — without you remembering to text"
